Media

The role of the media is to promote the team's achievements and events. They manage the team's Youtube, Instagram, TikTok, and even this website. They direct and produce a TV show called S.T.E.M. Talk, which talks about different aspects of S.T.E.M. in our community.

S.T.E.M. Talk

At Fire we utilize Livingston High School's state of the art T.V. studio to put on a 30 minute T.V. show called S.T.E.M. Talk. S.T.E.M. Talk is broadcast live in Livingston on Comcast 34 and Verizon 26 then put on the Fire YouTube channel. We use our show to spread awareness about S.T.E.M. in our community in an interactive and engaging way.
